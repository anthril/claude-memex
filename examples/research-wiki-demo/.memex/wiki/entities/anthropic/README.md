# Anthropic

## What it is

An AI safety company running an interpretability research programme, among other efforts. Known for the Claude model family, constitutional AI, and the transformer circuits agenda.

- Published the "Transformer Circuits Thread" covering induction heads, superposition, and related topics
- Applies circuit-level understanding toward safety goals (referred to in the source article as "deep ablation")
