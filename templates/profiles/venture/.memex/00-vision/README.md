# 00 — Vision

The first sketch of what we think we're building and who for. This is a
*sketch*, not a spec. The purpose is to make our guesses explicit so they
can be tested.

Curriculum source: COMP1100 Ch. 1 + Ch. 3 ("Define our hypotheses → sketch
our vision").

## Required artifacts

| File | Skill |
|---|---|
| `vision-sketch.md` | `venture-core/vision-sketch` |
| `day-in-life.md` | `venture-core/vision-sketch` |

## What "vision" means here

Three questions answered:

1. What are our customers' top problems?
2. Does our idea help solve them?
3. What does a day-in-the-life look like before vs after?

That's it. No roadmap, no feature list, no tech stack. Those come later
once hypotheses get tested.
